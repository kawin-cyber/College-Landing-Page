About TIGE

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1982

Located in the heart of Neo-Tokyo, the Tokyo Institute of Global Excellence stands at the intersection of rich cultural heritage and cutting-edge technological innovation.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of leaders, engineers, and artists to shape a sustainable and technologically advanced future. With world-class faculty, state-of-the-art research facilities, and a diverse global community, TIGE is truly the world's premier destination for higher education.
